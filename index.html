Introducing Delwin Portillo

Hello everyone! My name is Delwin Portillo and I am super excited to begin my journalist journey here in NYC! I have no background in journalism, but have a strong passion for it. Prior to this, I was a salesman where I discovered my love for storytelling and data. Below is a list of my journalist interests.

  • Data
  • Politics
  • Arts & Culture
  • Video

Below is a list of 3 of my favorite journalism stories and a brief description about why!

  • Consider the Lobster I love how David Foster Wallace uses humor and self-deprecation to gain trust with the audience. Reminds me of a written version of Last Week Tonight with John Oliver.
  • MultiLevel Marketing Speaking of John Oliver, this is one of my top episodes of his and a great piece of journalism. Him and his team do a great job at breaking down big overarching stories and simplifying them for everyone. He took this story a step further and even had it translated to spanish since MLM's have a big impact on the hispanic community.
  • The Worst Update in Youtube History Following my trend of video journalism, in this video Taylor Lorenz does a great job at the breaking down the dangers of data surveillance in the guise of safety. She does a great job pointing out how we got to the point that Youtube is asking millions for age verification, and the risks that come with that.
